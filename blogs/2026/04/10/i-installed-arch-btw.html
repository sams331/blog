Life I Installed Arch (btw) April 10, 2026 | sams331

This was actually (not that) a long time ago but I have the time to write it today. At that time I was bored and I remember I have Arch ISO installed into my USB, so I decided to install it to my laptop. This may be controversial but I think arch's installation is more straightforward than the 2 distros I have used, which is Mint and Debian. Even though the installation is straightforward, Debian is still the stablest and have the most working things on my laptop.

So, I have this 3 OSes for 3 different uses:

  • Mint: School things
  • Debian: Chill
  • Arch: warzone

Also, because my WiFi is absolutely trash at that time, I used USB tethering with my cellular data, and I successfully wasted 2.75GB of cellular data. Is it a lot? Yes. Is it worth it? Also yes. My "hardwork" paid off. It was such a great experience to try to install one of the most meme'd distro in Linux.

The world of a curious mind in a site.
